# Product Vision — NutriFit

## Product objective
Build a professional, scalable mobile wellness platform that helps users improve daily habits across nutrition and exercise, supported by an AI coach.

## Problem being solved
Users often track meals, workouts, and progress in disconnected apps (or not at all), making consistency difficult. They also lack timely, personalized guidance that is easy to follow.

## Target users
- Adults interested in healthier daily routines
- Beginners who need structured guidance
- Intermediate users who want consolidated tracking and motivation
- Users seeking habit coaching without clinical framing

## Value proposition
NutriFit provides one integrated workflow:
1. Log meals and exercise quickly
2. Track progress through simple, meaningful metrics
3. Receive contextual AI coaching to sustain momentum

The product is designed for wellness outcomes and behavior support, not medical diagnosis.
