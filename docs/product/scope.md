# Product Scope — NutriFit

## MVP scope (in)
- User account basics (sign-in/profile baseline)
- Meal logging (time, food items, estimated nutrition fields)
- Exercise logging (type, duration, intensity)
- Progress dashboard (core trends and streak indicators)
- AI coach chat for wellness guidance and habit support
- Basic reminders and daily consistency nudges

## Out of MVP (not in)
- Clinical-grade recommendations or diagnosis
- Direct insurance/provider workflows
- Full social network features (feeds, groups, challenges at scale)
- Complex wearable synchronization beyond initial exploration
- Advanced meal image recognition pipelines

## Future scope (post-MVP)
- Deeper personalization and adaptive coaching plans
- Health Connect / HealthKit integrations
- Smart goals with periodized exercise and nutrition plans
- Coach memory and long-horizon context optimization
- Premium tier capabilities and advanced analytics
