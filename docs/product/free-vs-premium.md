# Free vs Premium — NutriFit

## Tier definition

### Free (core wellness)
The following remain free:

- Meal logging
- Water logging
- Progress tracking
- Goals
- Exercise logging
- Dashboard
- Basic reminders

### Premium AI ($4.99/month)
The following require an active monthly subscription:

- AI nutrition coach
- Personalized recommendations
- Menu adjustments/refinements
- Conversational follow-up and continuity
- Voice conversation with premium limits

## Experience principles
- Free users can complete full tracking loops without paywalling fundamentals.
- Premium unlocks guidance quality and personalization depth, not basic data entry.
- Any premium upsell copy must frame value as wellness coaching, not medical outcomes.

## Launch scope note
- Premium AI launch includes text chat and turn-based voice guidance.
- Voice remains gated by separate premium limits to protect cost and quality of service.

## Initial monthly usage limits (conservative)
To protect margins in early rollout, propose:

1. **AI coaching messages:** 300 premium AI messages/month
2. **Personalized recommendation generations:** 60/month
3. **Menu adjustment actions:** 40/month
4. **AI voice turns:** 60 premium voice turns/month

### Why these limits
- High enough for regular weekly usage
- Low enough to cap outlier costs while real usage data is collected
- Simple to communicate and meter server-side

## Limit behavior
- Usage counters are tied to the subscriber billing cycle.
- Counters reset at each monthly renewal.
- Unused quota does not roll over.
- When quota is reached, app should provide:
  - clear explanation,
  - next reset date,
  - option to continue using free core features.
